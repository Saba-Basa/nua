READING
===================================

1) WHAT IS IT?
--------------
What is the thing being described?
(Name + type: concept, method, process, object, rule)

2) WHAT IS ITS PURPOSE?
-----------------------
What problem does it solve?
What is it supposed to achieve?

3) WHERE DOES IT BELONG?
-----------------------
How does it relate to other parts?
What comes before and after it (if applicable)?

4) WHEN DOES IT APPLY?
---------------------
Under what conditions is it used?
What situation triggers it?

5) WHAT ARE ITS LIMITS?
----------------------
What is it NOT responsible for?
What does it explicitly exclude?

6) WHAT MUST BE TRUE?
--------------------
What guarantees or conditions must hold?
What constraints does it operate under?

7) WHAT CAN CHANGE?
------------------
What depends on context, data, or configuration?
